# Paradise Removals - Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

### Logo
- **Type**: Circular icon with palm tree, golden sun, ocean waves, and sandy beach
- **File**: `/public/images/logo.jpg`
- **Usage**: Always display with adequate spacing; do not distort proportions

### Slogan
**"Make Your Move a Breeze"**

### Tone of Voice
- Professional yet warm and approachable
- Tropical/Gold Coast personality
- Trustworthy and reliable
- Friendly, never corporate

## Typography

### Headings: Sora
- **Font**: [Sora](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Sora)
- **Weights**: 400, 500, 600, 700, 800
- **Usage**: All headings (h1-h6), stats, badges

### Body: Outfit
- **Font**: [Outfit](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit)
- **Weights**: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700
- **Usage**: Body text, descriptions, form labels

## Design Patterns

### Section Backgrounds (alternating)
1. Navy gradient (Hero)
2. White (Services)
3. Navy gradient (Why Choose Us)
4. Sand Light (Service Areas)
5. White (About)
6. Navy gradient (Testimonials)
7. Sand Light (Quote Form)
8. Dark Navy (Footer)

### Wave Dividers
SVG wave shapes separate each section. Three variants available:
- `wave1` - Gentle single curve
- `wave2` - Double wave
- `wave3` - Asymmetric wave

### Buttons
- **Primary (Gold)**: Golden gradient with hover shimmer effect
- **Secondary (Outline)**: White border, transparent background

### Cards
- Rounded corners (1rem)
- Subtle border and shadow
- Gold left-border accent on hover
- Lift effect on hover (-6px translateY)

## Photography Style
- Professional team in navy uniforms
- Gold Coast settings (skyline, suburban homes)
- Branded moving boxes visible
- Warm, natural lighting
- Approachable and friendly mood
